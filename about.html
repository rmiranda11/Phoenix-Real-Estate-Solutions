About Our Founder

Earnest McElwee

Originally in real estate, a friend asked for his help with some inspections and Earnest was a natural. A year later and an untimely death of a close friend led Earnest to make a sudden decision: whether to continue with real estate or to expand into his true passion. He chose the latter and 15 years later the passion grew even stronger. Earnest specializes with first time buyers, but also has the knowledge to guide even the most seasoned clients. He keeps himself up to date by teaching seminars in the Chicagoland Area to a variety of potential buyers. He is a true people person and loves to keep a simple yet upbeat dialogue to ensure that you are informed but not bombarded with information that will inevitably be provided in your report at the end of the inspection.
